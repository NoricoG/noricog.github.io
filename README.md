## Welcome 👋

I like making simple websites.
No backend, no accounts, no cookies, no tracking, no ads.

Do you want to browse wikipedia? [WikiWander](https://noricog.github.io/wiki-wander/)

Do you want to guess wikipedia pages? [Redactle Turbo](https://noricog.github.io/redactle-turbo/)

Do you want to play a binary puzzle? [Binori](https://noricog.github.io/binori/)

Do you want to make music? [Wirbel](https://noricog.github.io/wirbel/)

Do you want to see geometric images and animations? [Geometrico](https://github.com/NoricoG/geometrico)

Do you want to simulate evolution? [Evoluto](https://noricog.github.io/evolution/)
